Best Type of Wallpapers for Residential Houses

Mural Wallpapers

The mural wallpaper is ideal for bringing vitality and breaking up monotony in a place to prevent drabness. Using various arts, you can hang it in the kids’ room, corridor, living room, bedroom, etc.. Mural wallpapers have the amusing feature that you can choose the kind of art you want for your background.

Kshs.1,500 – to Kshs.3,500 per roll

Floral Wallpapers

The usage of flower wallpaper for your house is another popular trend; when you touch them, your space exudes an inviting, natural vibe

Kshs.1,500 – to Kshs.3,500 per roll

Textured Wallpapers

A wall can occasionally be made to appear as though it is composed of a natural, textured substance by using textured wallpaper.

Kshs.1,500 – to Kshs.3,500 per roll

Contact papers

For those of you unfamiliar, the product is smooth and often has a sticky surface on one side and a decorative surface on the other side. The paper adheres to the target surface effortlessly. The ideal places to use contact paper are on kitchen counters, to cover a refrigerator, to make do-it-yourself furniture, to brighten up a stairway, etc

Kshs.1,500 – to Kshs.3,500 per roll
