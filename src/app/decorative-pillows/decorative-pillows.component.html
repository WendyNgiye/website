Types of Throw Pillows to Decorate Your Home With

Common Types of Throw Pillow Shapes

Square Pillows: The All-Around All-Star

The square pillow, a classic throw pillow style, is the cornerstone of interior design, versatile and timeless. Whether it’s a petite 16-inch for a subtle accent or a generous 24-inch Euro pillow to anchor your living space, these pillows are the go-to for a reason. Wrapped in a luxurious fabric cover or boasting a simple, solid color, the square pillow easily adapts to any style. It’s a reliable choice for a quick living room refresh or as a staple in your throw pillow collection.

Lumbar Pillows: The Supportive Statement

Lumbar pillows, a popular type of accent pillow, offer a stylish solution to comfort with their elongated elegance. They work wonders on a deep sofa, as a statement piece on a window seat, or as a final touch to a guest bed, bringing both form and function to your space.

Round Pillows: The Soft Accent

Round pillows are a delightful departure from the traditional. Their smooth curves bring a unique visual softness to any decor. These charming orbs defy the convention of straight lines, introducing a playful element to rigid furniture pieces. Perfect for nestling into a reading corner or adding a touch of whimsy to a child’s bedroom, round pillows are as functional as they are decorative. The circular shape of a round pillow offers a welcome deviation, inviting creativity in arrangement and styling.

Bolster Pillows: The Versatile Classic

Elongated and elegant, bolster pillows add a distinctive touch to traditional and contemporary spaces. Often found gracing the ends of luxurious sofas or the center of a majestic bed, these pillows combine practical support with sophisticated style
