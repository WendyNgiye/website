Ideas for Designing the Closet of Your Dreams

Add a Dresser

If you have a custom and spacious closet , you'll have plenty of room to accommodate a dresser.This design features not only a lot of hanging storage space, but also offers drawers for all of your folded clothes.

Create a Place for Shoes/shoerack

Optimize your closet space by dedicating a home for everything including adding a shoerack .you need to store in a way that makes it easy to sort through and see exactly what you have.

Add Space in a Standard Closet

Just because you don't have a walk-in closet doesn't mean you can't benefit from a gorgeous custom closet organizing system

Create a Place for Accessories

Make your closet functional by featuring a vanity and drawers that are perfect for stashing all of your accessories, jewelry, and makeup.

Use Baskets

An easy way to add extra storage and keep your closet space tidy is by adding baskets, Baskets are perfect for random articles that don't have another home .

Add Lighting

Don't underestimate the value of a wonderful light fixture.hanging light fictures can elevate your space and give it that custom look and feel.

Add a Table

If you have the space, adding a table to the center of your closet can be a great way to add a landing for folding clothes. A side chair is also a great alternative to add a touch of space for organizing.
