Types of Air Diffusers & Fresheners

Essential Oil Diffusers

Essential oil diffusers use ultrasonic technology to disperse natural oils into the air, filling your space with a calming or energizing fragrance. They also often double as humidifiers, adding moisture to the air.

$30 - $80

Reed Diffusers

Reed diffusers consist of scented oils absorbed by reeds, slowly releasing fragrance into the air without the need for heat or electricity. They are perfect for continuous, low-maintenance scenting.

$30 - $80

Aerosol Air Fresheners

Aerosol air fresheners are easy-to-use sprays that provide immediate freshness. They are portable and perfect for quick bursts of fragrance in any room.

$30 - $80
